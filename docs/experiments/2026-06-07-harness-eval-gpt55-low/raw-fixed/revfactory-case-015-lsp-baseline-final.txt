Implemented the MiniLang LSP server in `src/server.mjs`.

Included:
- JSON-RPC `Content-Length` framing and streaming decode
- `initialize`, `initialized`, `shutdown`, `exit`
- permissive MiniLang parsing with comments and error recovery
- symbols for functions, params, variables
- diagnostics for parse issues, duplicate functions, undefined symbols/functions, wrong arity
- completion, go-to-definition, hover
- `didOpen` and incremental `didChange`
